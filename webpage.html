AVENGERS

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) films are a series of American superhero films
produced by Marvel Studios based on characters that appear in publications by Marvel Comics.
The MCU is the shared universe in which all of the films are set.
The films have been in production since 2007, and in that time Marvel Studios has produced and released 23 films,
with at least 14 more in various stages of development. It is the highest-grossing film franchise of all time,
having grossed over $22.5 billion at the global box office.
